int Gatherable.GatherableItem.chance

The chance to drop this one (Added to the all others chance and generate a random throught them).

Considering a pool of 3 items whith 10, 30 and 20 of chance respectively. A random number between 0 and 60 (exclusive) will be generated, and then, we will get the right item.

–From 0 to 9 : The first item

–From 10 to 39 : The second item

–From 40 to 59 : The third item
